Tons of Memories

Meeting you changed my life in a colorful way. You broke my silly belief about Online love and transformed how I see it. Before, I thought love was just about being with someone, but when I met you, I realized what it means to have commitments. You've brought joy and purpose into my dull life. Every moment with you will always be a treasured memory, and you've shown me the true meaning of being with someone you love and I am so grateful for you.
Recently, I was really mentally exhausted and broken, unable to put in that simple trust and belief you wanted (I know it wasn't anything out of the blue). But now, feeling this emptiness, I can't help but look at your pictures and pray for you to become an even better person. So wishing you a very Happy Birthday to you Baby.

Our First Date

Our first Movie date on 30.10.2023 was unforgettable. We went to see the 12th Fail movie, and it was such a fun & motivating experience. It was my first Hindi movie in cinemas, and you made it even more special. I remember the excitement and joy of watching it with you(and ahem). We laughed about random stuffs during the interval, which somehow added to the fun of the day. It's a memory I will always cherish.
I remember looking at your smile and your nagging to take my mask off while feeling how lucky i was to have gotten a chance to be the love of your life in this boring life of mine. I remember how the first thing I did was hug you out of happiness behind the cricket court and how we were so sad with that goodbye. So, remembering all these moments, I wish you a very happy birthday, my Big Babla ji.

We Didn't just Vibe

We used to spend hours on Calls, and every day that passed without double tick showing not showing now on Whatsapp made me so sad and regretful. I miss mera jealous hona Aadya se fir ghanto baatein karna PJ ke padhte time. Those moments are unforgettable, and they make me realize how special our time together on call was.

We didn't just vibe; we stayed with each other, giving company even when things got boring. It wasn't the fun we had on call but the sense of home we felt with each other's company and voice. So, a BIG THANKS for everything you have done for me. All I would like is to talk or give it a start maybe.

Our Life

From our first date to the last and all the time we spent talking with each other still feels like a bliss to me and I know for a fact you feel the same except got the regret that I made things end a way they werent supposed to. Just because something has to end doesn't mean that it has to end badly. From your scent to your smile and from holding hands to kissing you, everything still feels as real to me as we did on the first time except now its all in my imagination.
I said I regret choosing you over a fight and I realised that one line like that is enough to shake someone up from the very bottom when you get to hear "I regret saying yes to you". All the mean things I said shouldnt damp down your birthday so I wont text a single time on your birth day. Enjoy your day and don't forget to remember me atleast once. Please.
